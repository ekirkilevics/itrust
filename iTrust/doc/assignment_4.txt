One "design decision" I made was regarding the timestamps when the HCP views a list of the patients he or she
is monitoring. The acceptance test made it seem like times should appear with no seconds and AM & PM, however, 
following the pattern in the rest of iTrust, I decided to leave these in military time with a second count.
The requirements for UC34 say a "timestamp" should be displayed so to me, converting the timestamp automatically
to text is the best interpretation of this requirement. My tests reflect this assumption.
